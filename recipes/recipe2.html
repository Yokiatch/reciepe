Choorma

Desi ghee ka choorma is a dish mostly famous in haryana,rajasthan and punjab.The reason is simple.These three states are among the highest desi ghee producer or dairy products

Ingredients

  • 2-3 chapatis (Depends on your appetite)
  • Desi ghee
  • Jaggery (You can also choose brown sugar)

Process

  1. Take 2-3 hot chapatis in a plate
  2. Crush the chapatis just like brown sugar
  3. Boil the desi ghee
  4. Break the jaggery into smaller pieces
  5. Add the jaggery pieces and boiled ghee as per your requirements
  6. Mix all the ingredient
  7. Your desi ghee ka choorma is ready

2 Mins Maggie

Description

This is a recipe of one of my favourite dishes.The reason this is my favourite dish is that it is easy to cook,takes less ingredient and it only 5-10 minutes to cook

Ingredients

  • A maggie packet
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • Maggie Masala
  • Toppings(carrot,peas,caulifloweror potato) depends on your preferences

Process

  1. Light up the stove
  2. Take a pot and put it on stove
  3. Add a tablespoon of ghee
  4. Open the maggie packet and put all the noodles inside the pot
  5. Stir the noodles for 20 seconds
  6. Add a glass of water
  7. Stir the water until the noodles are loosened
  8. Now, Add the maggie masala and mix it nicely
  9. You can also add some toppings if you want to
  10. Let the water boil completely
  11. If the maggie starts sticking under the lid of the pot turn off the stove and keep stirring it until it cools down
  12. Your maggie is ready to eat

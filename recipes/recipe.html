Besan Ki chilla

Besan ka chilla is a famous high protein indian dish.It is also one of easy to make indian dish.

Ingredients

  • Besan(Gram Flour)
  • Salt
  • Ajwain
  • turmeric
  • Chopped Onions,Tomatoes,Grated Ginger and Green chillies
  • Coriander Leaves

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup besan,salt as needed,half teaspoon ajwain to a mixing bowl
  2. Then add finely chopped onions,tomatoes,,grated ginger and green chillies.Make sure onions and tomatoes are very finely chopped
  3. Add coriander leaves and pour 3/4 leaves
  4. Mix gently and make a batter of thick but poring consistency
  5. Add more water if needed.I used little less than 1 cup.If the batter is too thick then the chilla will not cook properly and may cause stomach upset
  6. Heat a tawa or griddle.If not using non stick pan, then grease it well with with few drops of oil.When it is hot enough,pour one ladle full of batter
  7. Spread it as thin as possible
  8. Regulate the flame to medium and drizzle 1/2 to 3/4 tsp of oil or ghee
  9. When the edge start leaving the pan flip the batter and repeate the same process
  10. When you see brown spots on both sides put chilla in another plate
  11. Server Besan ka chilla with tea,chutney or pickle
